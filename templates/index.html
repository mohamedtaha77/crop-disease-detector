AI-Based Crop Disease Detection

This web application uses a deep learning model trained on plant leaf images to detect crop diseases. Upload a photo of a leaf, and the model will analyze it using Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to classify the disease affecting the crop. This tool helps farmers and researchers detect diseases early and act faster.
